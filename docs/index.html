Farah Nadia Jamalludin

Teacher. Writer. Poet. Game Developer.

I’m a Malay language teacher with the Ministry of Education, Singapore. A Malay quatrain (pantun) enthusiast, I first participated in NUS Pesta Pantun in 2006 and is now involved regularly as jury for NUS Pesta Pantun, Singapore Polytechnic’s SPantun, and have also once judged a regional pantun competition by Temasya Pantun Piala Seri Melaka - PISMA (Malaysia). I also build my own teaching resources such as card games and board games for Malay language learning.
